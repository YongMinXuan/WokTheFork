Our FAQ Page

How do I reserve my seats and do I have to book in advance?

You can do so by filling in our booking form hereAdvance bookings can be made for 7 days.

Can I get a refund if I need to?

Unfortunately, We do not accept any requests for refunds.

What if I have Special Dietary Needs?

Do Indicate in the booking form that you have special dietary concerns. We will make the necessary arrangements for you to not be sent to the hospital and yet enjoy the Singaporean Cuisine to the fullest.

What happens in case of bad weather?

We provide full transport to all areas of the tour. The buses will stop at a sheltered area for you to alight. Furthermore, We have provided a disposable raincoat as well as an umbrella in the welfare pack.

How will we get tot our destinations?

Full transport will be provided. Buses will be arranged to ferry you to the places. In the case of the destinations being close to one another,our tour guides will show you the way.
